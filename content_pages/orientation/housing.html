Housing Resources

Many students live on-campus or in Evanston. Some students rent apartments in Chicago and the neighboring suburbs. Since the campus is centrally located, public transportation is excellent. It is highly advisable for students who wish to rent apartments to visit the area and make living arrangements before beginning their graduate work.

The Off-Campus Housing Office

The Off-Campus Housing office is located in Scott Hall on the Evanston campus. The Off-Campus website contains the information and resources available to students living off-campus, from apartment hunting to being a good neighbor within the Chicago/Evanston and Northwestern communities.
